Non-Invasive Continuous Blood Pressure Monitoring
08/2022 - 05/2023

  • Sponsor & Organization: NASA and NIH-funded research conducted in conjunction with the Neural Systems Group BME Lab (Massachusetts General Hospital and Harvard Med) Platform: Arduino Software Used: Solidworks Language: C++ Algo: PID Placing the device around the superficial temporal artery in front of the cephalic ear of ICU patients to perform measuring the diastolic and systolic blood pressures by controlling the extrusion pressure and utilizing an applanation tonometry pressure sensor.
